Hello everyone!

First, thank you so much for being here.  
Since starting this publication, I've mostly focused on reflections around movies, series, and storytelling — a space I truly love.

But lately, I’ve been feeling the need to broaden what I share here.  
I'm passionate not only about culture, but also about communication strategy, UX design, productivity systems, and the evolving digital world we all live in.

Moving forward, you’ll still find culture reviews here (especially on Fridays), but you’ll also see thoughtful insights on digital life, privacy, tools for work, and creative strategies for navigating this fast-changing landscape.

My goal is simple:

> **To offer you reflections that help you think, work, and live better — across both stories and systems.**

Additionally, I’m expanding my work beyond publishing into **consulting**.  
I help brands and professionals grow by improving how they **communicate**, **present**, and **operate** — through better messaging, strategic design, and digital optimization.

If you enjoy my perspective and would like to collaborate — whether for a project, brand consultation, or workflow improvement — feel free to reach out.  
I'd love to hear from you.

Thank you again for reading and growing with me.  
I'm excited to keep building this space — and to make it even more valuable, practical, and inspiring.

If you know someone who’d enjoy these topics, feel free to share this publication with them.

— Felipe
