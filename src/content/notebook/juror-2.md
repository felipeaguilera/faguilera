# **Synopsis**

Justin Kemp, a journalist and recovering alcoholic, is unexpectedly selected as a juror for a high-profile murder trial in Georgia. The case revolves around a young woman found dead at the bottom of a ravine outside a bar, where she had an intense argument with her boyfriend the night before. Initially, the case seems straightforward, but as Justin hears the testimonies, he realizes he was at the same bar that night and fears he might be involved in an accident that led to the woman's death. Caught in a moral dilemma and facing a determined prosecutor, Justin desperately seeks the truth to prevent the conviction of an innocent man and avoid a devastating outcome for himself and his family.
