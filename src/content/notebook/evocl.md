## From Felipe Aguilera to the Universe: Substack Edition.

I am endlessly immersed in culture, from movies and TV series to both fiction and non-fiction books, and even music. The breadth of humanity's creative output is truly astounding, and I am continually in awe of what has been produced and what the future holds. My passion for cultural consumption is unceasing, and I anticipate it will continue until my final days.

However, the most fulfilling aspect of this passion is not just the consumption but the sharing, discussing, and communicating of these experiences. I hold a sincere hope that my interests can enrich others' lives, whether it be for entertainment, knowledge, or as part of a collective endeavor.

### Why Substack?

I'm aiming to connect with a new English-speaking audience, adopt a fresh format, and most importantly, establish a consistent publishing routine. This platform appears to be the perfect fit for these three goals.

My objective is to publish several posts weekly. I'm exploring the most effective best practices to optimize this process. I believe setting a weekly project challenge for myself is an excellent strategy. At present, this project is accessible to all, but I aspire to develop exclusive, more comprehensive projects in the future.
