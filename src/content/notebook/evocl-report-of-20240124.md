*This week I’ve watched a couple of good movies, trying to catch what I missed in the cinemas, like Anatomy of a Fall and MEN, but also a quite surprising new-to-me director from Finland.*

# 🎥🔍🦮 Anatomy of a Fall (2023)

I have a soft spot for mysteries, especially those that include a trial, which adds extra intrigue. This film comes highly recommended for these reasons and more. It's not merely a mystery; it's a drama depicting a European couple's struggle to balance their dreams, fears, and sacrifices. They confront severe internal and external family issues with openness. Directed by Justine Triet, it's an intense and comprehensive journey that is well worth the watching. A special nod must be given to Sandra Hüller's outstanding performance and, of course, to Messi, the dog.

#### ⭐ 9 of 10, Available on [Prime Video](https://t.co/EXK0mmBR0E).

---

# 🎥🌿MEN (2022)

I hold Alex Garland in high esteem, primarily because of *Ex Machina*, and though not as much, *Annihilation*. On this occasion, this British horror film “Men”, starring Harper, a widow seeking tranquility in a quiet British village, stands out. Both the location and the cinematography make it a truly captivating film, even with its disturbing ending. However, in this installment, the conflict, script, and development suffer from a lack of clarity, depth, and an overly forced “artistic” quality, in a negative sense.

#### ⭐ 6 of 10, Available on [Prime Video](https://www.primevideo.com/detail/0OVLKOE1N1RT0C5B4H900PQ18H/ref=atv_dp_share_cu_r).

---

# 🎥🍂 Fallen Leaves (2023)

I had no idea about the Finnish director Aki Kaurismäki, which gave me a surprising feeling that there was something strange—a presentation devoid of emotions and expressions. This decision ultimately turned this romantic comedy into an adorable, fascinating, and unique experience. Like good European cinema, it’s quite different and possibly not for everyone, but definitely a great way to see something new.

In Helsinki, Fallen Leaves allows us to witness the encounter between Ansa and Holappa, both characters with multiple fears, stubbornness, and a touch of foolishness. They try to thrive and get to know each other amidst a series of absurd missteps.

#### ⭐ 8 of 10, Available on [MUBI](https://mubi.com/en/cl/films/fallen-leaves-2023).
