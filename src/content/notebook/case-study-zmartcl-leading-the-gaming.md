# **Overview**

For over a decade, I led the communication, customer experience, and marketing strategy at Zmart, Chile's most recognized retail chain for video games, board games, and geek culture. As Client Experience and Marketing Manager, I helped shape a brand that resonated with enthusiasts and newcomers alike.

# **The Context**

Zmart wasn’t just a store — it was a hub for gamers. We worked closely with top-tier global brands like Nintendo, PlayStation, Xbox, Razer, Asmodee, and Devir to bring the latest in gaming to Chilean customers. Our mission: make gaming accessible, exciting, and community-driven.

# **The Challenge**

We needed to:

- Deliver a standout omnichannel experience (physical + e-commerce)
- Guide both hardcore gamers and first-time buyers
- Create a unique tone and identity in customer support and brand voice
- Compete with giant retailers while staying lean

# **My Role & Contributions**

I led both the **design team** and the **customer support team**, developing the entire communication strategy:

- Designed and executed major campaigns in collaboration with Nintendo, PlayStation, Xbox, and others
- Directed social media and customer service messaging tone
- Managed visual content (print magazine, POP material, digital assets)
- Oversaw email campaigns, product launches, and promotional strategy
- Balanced budget management with performance metrics

# **Results & Achievements**

- **+240% revenue growth** over 3 years by expanding into tabletop gaming and geek culture
- **#1 Day-One Console Sales** in Chile for launches of PlayStation 4, Nintendo Switch, Xbox Series X
- Outperformed large retailers like Falabella, Paris, and Ripley in key gaming launches
- **Top follower engagement** in Chile’s gaming retail space
- Named **"Great Place to Work" 3 years in a row**

## **Why It Matters**

Zmart proved that a small, passionate team could lead with creativity, strategy, and culture. We didn’t just sell games — we built trust, excitement, and a lasting identity.

## **What I Learned**

- Strategic consistency across channels is more powerful than big budgets
- A lean, mission-aligned team can beat the odds
- Listening to the community builds loyalty and long-term value

> # “Everyone likes to play. The key is helping them discover how.”
